Hello! My name is Luca and this is my story

When me and my parents moved to Nairobi, the capital of Kenya, I was reading books about andangered animals. So many of the animals people associate with Africa live here: cheetahs, lions, zebras and elephants.

While reading, I discovered that all these animals were endangered and could extinct in a few years. The forests all around Kenya are being cut down to make way for big farms. This means that the habitats of all these animals are being destroyed by humans.

I began to think what simple things I could do to protect these animals and their habitats.

The money I raised doing this meant I could also buy books for schools that could afford them. These books would help teach other children about endangered animals, like they had for me, and inspire the next bunch of animal and conservation activists. It might seem like a mountain to climb but beimg an animal activist is easy as recycling paper and cartons.
